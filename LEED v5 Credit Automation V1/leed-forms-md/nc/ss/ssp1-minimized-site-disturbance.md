### Compliance by permit

Confirm that the project site is at least one acre and located in an area where the EPA is the NPDES permitting authority, OR the project is subject, by law, to the EU Taxonomy: DNSH, Pollution Prevention, Item 4 Noise and Dust.

### Compliance by local equivalent

Confirm that the local standard/code is being applied and is equivalent to or more stringent than the 2022 U.S. EPA CGP or EU Taxonomy: DNSH, Pollution Prevention, Item 4 Noise and Dust.

Provide the name of the local standard/code being applied (if applicable).
