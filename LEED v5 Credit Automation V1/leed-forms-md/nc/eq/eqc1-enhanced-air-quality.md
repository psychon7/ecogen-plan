### **Option 1.** Increased ventilation

1

The project team has increased breathing zone outdoor air ventilation rates of at least 95% of all regularly occupied spaces by at least 15% above the minimum rates, as determined in EQp Fundamental Air Quality. Compliance with this requirement is shown within EQp Fundamental Air Quality.

OR

### **Option 2.** Enhanced indoor air quality design

1

The design compounds and design limits outlined in ASHRAE 62.1-2022, Tables 6-5 and 6-6 have been met. In addition, IAQP design limits of 10 ug/m3 for PM2.5, 20 ug/m3 for formaldehyde, and 10 ppb for ozone have been met. Compliance with this requirement is shown within EQp Fundamental Air Quality.
