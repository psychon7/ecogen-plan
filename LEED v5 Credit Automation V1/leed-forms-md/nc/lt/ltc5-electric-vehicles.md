### Accessible parking space confirmation

Confirm that at least one EVSE is located in an accessible parking space at least 9-feet (2.5-meters) wide with a 5-foot (1.5-meters) access aisle and has accessibility features for use by persons with mobility, ambulatory, and visual limitations.

EVSE specifications

Provide evidence that the EVSE meet all the following criteria:
 • Provides level 2 or 3 charging capacity per the manufacturer's requirements and the requirements of the National Electrical Code (NFPA 70)
 • Provides 208–240 volts or greater for each required space
 • Meet the connected functionality criteria for ENERGY STAR® -certified EVSE and be capable of responding to time-of-use market signals (e.g., price)Upload documentation
