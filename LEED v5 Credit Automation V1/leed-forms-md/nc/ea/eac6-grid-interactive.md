### Confirm compliance with the following:

The project design includes electric meters with interval recording capabilities and all grid-interactive equipment within the project scope can receive an external signal, directly or indirectly, to indicate when load management should be enabled.

The technology and controls in place for energy storage, demand response, automated demand-side management, and power resiliency, as applicable, are documented in the project systems manual, or Current Facilities Requirements (CFR) and Operations and Maintenance (OM) plan.

Confirm that grid-interactive equipment and controls are included in the commissioning scope of work and clearly indicated in the commissioning documentation provided for *EAp3: Fundamental Commissioning*.
