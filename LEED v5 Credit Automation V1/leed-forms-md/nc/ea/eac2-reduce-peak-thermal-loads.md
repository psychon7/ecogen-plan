### Excluded systems

Identify exclusions for systems not meeting the ERV or HRV criteria (including bypass or heat exchanger override controls requirements and minimum 70% enthalpy recovery ratio or 75% sensible heat recovery ratio):

Fan systems that in aggregate supply less than 15% of the project’s total outdoor air.

Kitchen exhaust demand ventilation systems. All excluded systems meet the provisions of ASHRAE 90.1-2019 or 90.1-2022 Section 6.5.7.2.3(b), even if exhaust volumes are too low to trigger this provision.

Laboratory exhaust systems. All excluded systems meet the provisions of ASHRAE 90.1-2019 or 90.1-2022 Section 6.5.7.3 (a) or (b), even if exhaust volumes are too low to trigger this provision.
