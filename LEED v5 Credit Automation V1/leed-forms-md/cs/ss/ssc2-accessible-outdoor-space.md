Vegetated outdoor space calculation

Enter the percentage of the accessible outdoor space that is vegetated and planted with two or more types of vegetation or have overhead vegetated canopy. At least 25% of the minimum required outdoor space (30% of the total site area) is required.

Outdoor space calculation

Enter the percentage of the site area that includes barrier-free and physically accessible outdoor space for persons with limited mobility. At least 30% of the total site area (including building footprint) is required.
