### **Option 1.** Renewable supply or procurement

4

### Contract length

Confirm ownership of the annual renewable generation for a minimum of 10 years. For a duration shorter than ten years or a one-time bulk purchase, confirm the annual renewable energy quantity allocated to the project is determined by prorating the total contracted EACs or RECs across ten years.

### Environmental attributes

Confirm that all environmental attributes associated with renewable energy generation are retained by the project.

- For Tier 1, EACs or RECs are not sold, transferred, or arbitraged with off-site renewable energy.
- For Tier 2 or Tier 3, EACs or RECs are not arbitraged with other off-site renewable energy that does not meet the criteria for the selected Tier.

Shared renewable systems

For any shared attributes, provide documentation from the owner’s representative confirming the capacity/generation that is allocated to the project building and confirming that this capacity/generation will not double count toward any other properties than the referenced LEED project.
