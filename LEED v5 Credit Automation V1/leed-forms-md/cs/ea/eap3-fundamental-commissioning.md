Commissioning report

Provide the final or draft commissioning (Cx) report. If the report is a draft, include a list of incomplete tasks, and a plan for the completion of commissioning and training addressing climatic and other conditions required for performance of any deferred tests. Address commissioning of the envelope or building enclosure (BECx) in the Cx report or provide a stand-alone BECx report. Ensure the following sections and all report content required in the referenced commissioning standard are included in the Cx Report:

- Owner’s Project Requirements (OPR) addressing envelope, HVAC, service water heating, power, lighting, and other equipment
- Basis of Design (BOD)
- Commissioning (Cx) Plan
- Functional performance tests. Include at least one completed performance test script for each applicable discipline, including HVAC, service water heating, power, lighting, and other equipment (specifically addressing on-site renewable systems if applicable)
- On-going Cx plan that allows building operators to maintain the project’s high performance and includes a set of blank forms for future use by the building operations and maintenance team

### Key tasks and milestones

Confirm completion of the following key tasks and milestones in addition to those required by ASHRAE 90.1 or equivalent standard, **and confirm that associated dates are recorded in the commissioning report:**

- **Commissioning provider (CxP) designation:** The owner designated the CxP by the end of the design development phase.
- **CxP review of OPR and BOD**
- **Design phase meeting(s):** The CxP participated in at least one coordination or design review meeting to discuss design review comments and commissioning of the building envelope, and of the mechanical, electrical, and plumbing systems.
- **Submittal review:** The CxP reviewed submittals for design deviations that impact the OPR.
- **QA/QC construction documentation:** The CxP performed QA/QC review of and at least 10% of the completed contractors’ documents, including checklists and functional tests.
- **Construction phase milestone meetings:** The CxP participated in 50% and 100% construction phase milestone meetings to discuss the commissioning findings and work toward resolving identified issues.
